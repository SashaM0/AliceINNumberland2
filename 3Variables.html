Abstraction

Pigeon claims that the little girl is actually some kind of snake, since both the little girl and snakes eat eggs. This statement is based on the general concept of abstraction, which is widespread in many fields of science, for example, in mathematics it is used to replace variables.

Abstraction in mathematics is the process of extracting the underlying structures, patterns or properties of a mathematical concept, removing any dependence on real world objects with which it might originally have been connected, and generalizing it
